Downgrading


Downgrading can be used to unpatch exploits and unenroll.

Prerequisites


You will need:
A USB flash drive with at least 8GB of storage
Another computer with access to the Chromebook Recovery Utility

Check kernver

On your chromebook, hold the ESC, Power, And Refresh keys for 1-2 seconds. Your chromebook should boot into a menu that looks something like this (it might take up to 20 seconds)
Or this


Next, press tab and you should see some text appear. Look for the text that says "kernver=" and then some numbers. If those numbers end in a 2, you can downgrade to ChromeOS 112 or above and can unenroll. If they end in a 3, you can only downgrade to ChromeOS 120 and above and can't unenroll.
